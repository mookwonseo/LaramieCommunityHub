Great news for parents! Registration for **Adventure Kids Summer Camp 2026** is opening soon with a new lottery system. Here are the important details you need to know:

### Key Registration Dates
- **Registration Opens:** Thursday, March 26th at 12:00 PM (Noon).
- **Registration Window:** Open all weekend. Since this is a lottery system, the time you sign up does not affect your selection.
- **Lottery Spin:** Monday, March 30th.

### What Happens Next?
Once the lottery is completed on Monday, the City of Laramie will send out confirmation emails to notify families of their status. 

### How to Sign Up
Registration will take place through the City of Laramie's online portal. Be sure to have your account ready before Thursday noon!
