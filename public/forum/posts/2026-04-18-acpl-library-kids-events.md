The **Albany County Public Library (ACPL)** offers a fantastic lineup of free programs for children and youth throughout April and May! Whether you have a toddler who loves stories or a grade-schooler interested in STEM, there's something for everyone.

### Regular Weekly Programs
- **Book Babies & Toddler Storytime**: Perfect for the littlest readers to build early literacy skills through songs, stories, and play.
- **Monday FunDay**: Engaging and educational afterschool activities for elementary students.
- **LEGO Club**: Collaborative building challenges that spark creativity and problem-solving.

### Special Upcoming Events
In addition to their weekly schedule, the library hosts special seasonal workshops, including the ongoing **Garden Series** and special craft sessions.

### How to Check the Schedule
For the most up-to-date monthly calendar, specific times for each age group, and to check if registration is required for any events, visit the official ACPL events page:

👉 **[ACPL Library Events Calendar](https://www.acplwy.org/event/library-events)**
