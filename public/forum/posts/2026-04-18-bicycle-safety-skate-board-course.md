The Laramie Recreation Center is offering a **Bicycle Safety and Skateboard Course** for youth! Registration for this program opens soon, so be sure to mark your calendars.

### Course Details
- **Registration Opens:** May 1, 2026
- **Registration Deadline:** July 30, 2026
- **Location:** Laramie Recreation Center

If you or your children are interested in learning or improving bicycle safety and skateboarding skills, this is a fantastic opportunity provided by the city.

### How to Register
You can browse the available youth programs and register online through the Laramie Parks & Rec portal:

👉 **[Register for Bicycle Safety & Skateboard Course](https://wylaramieweb.myvscloud.com/webtrac/web/search.html?module=AR&type=YOUTH)**
