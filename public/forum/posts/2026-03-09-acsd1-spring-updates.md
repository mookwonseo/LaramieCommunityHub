With spring now underway, here's a quick roundup of important upcoming dates and news from Albany County School District #1 for families in Laramie.

## Upcoming No-School Days

- **March 25 (Wed)** — Early Release, All Students
- **April 2–3 (Thu–Fri)** — Spring Break begins
- **April 6 (Mon)** — Spring Break continues (No School)

Check the full district calendar at **[www.acsd1.org](https://www.acsd1.org)** for the complete list of early releases and staff development days.

## School Meal Menus

The district's daily lunch and breakfast menus are updated on Nutrislice:

👉 [acsd1.nutrislice.com/menu](https://acsd1.nutrislice.com/menu)

Menus are updated weekly and include allergen information.

## Spring Sports & Activities Registration

Several spring youth programs are opening for registration soon:

- **MLS GO Soccer** — starts April 13 for grades K–7
- **Track & Field** — starts April 28 for grades 5–12
- **Little Kickers** — starts April 14 for ages 3–5

Registration is through the City of Laramie Recreation Department. See the [Schedule page](/schedule) for full details and times.
